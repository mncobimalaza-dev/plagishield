What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your submitted documents to train any AI model.
  • We do not share your document content with other users or third parties beyond the analysis providers listed above.

Data retention

Account data (name, email, hashed password) is kept until you delete your account. Document text submitted for analysis is not retained after your report is generated. Scan history summaries are capped at your most recent 20 scans.

Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use signed, HTTP-only cookies that cannot be read or forged by browser scripts. Our API key for the Claude API is stored server-side only and is never exposed to your browser.
